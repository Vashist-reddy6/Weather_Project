🌊 Flood Emergency

Flood Safety Steps
  1. Move immediately to higher ground — do not wait.
  2. Avoid walking in water over 15cm — it can knock you over.
  3. Disconnect all electrical appliances.
  4. Follow evacuation orders from local authorities.
  5. Do NOT drive through flooded roads.
  6. Store 3L water/person/day, food, torch, medicines.

Safe zones: School upper floors · Community halls · High-ground structures

🌀 Cyclone / Storm

Cyclone Safety Steps
  1. Stay indoors, away from windows and glass doors.
  2. Move to the innermost room on the lowest floor.
  3. Do NOT go outside during the eye — winds will return.
  4. Secure all outdoor items before the storm hits.
  5. Charge devices and fill water containers before power cuts.
  6. Monitor NDMA: ndma.gov.in or local radio.

☀️ Heatwave

Heatwave Safety
  1. Stay indoors 12 PM – 4 PM (peak heat).
  2. Drink water every 20 minutes.
  3. Wear loose, light-colored cotton clothing.
  4. Never leave children/elderly in parked vehicles.
  5. Heat stroke signs: hot skin + confusion → call 108.

⛈ Thunderstorm

Thunderstorm Safety
  1. Seek shelter in a solid building or hard-topped vehicle.
  2. Avoid open fields, hilltops, and isolated trees.
  3. Stay away from metal structures.
  4. Wait 30 minutes after the last thunder before going outside.
